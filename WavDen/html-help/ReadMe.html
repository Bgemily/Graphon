Welcome to Wavelet Denoising Toolbox for Matlab

We have implemented several procedures (scripts) to help you discover denoising properties of wavelets in various settings. There is an accompagnying paper that describes the mathematics behind each procedure available in the toolbox. Three extra matlab procedures, example.m simulexample.m and simulations.m are available in the toolbox. They contain the scripts that we have used for illustating the paper.
