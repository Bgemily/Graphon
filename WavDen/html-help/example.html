example

 

An example to illustrate two wavelet denoising procedures

 

DESCRIPTION

 
An example to illustrate two wavelet denoising procedures on an electrical consumption signal.

 

USAGE

 example

 

REQUIRED ARGUMENTS

             None

VALUE

 

             A figure displaying the original signal together with plots of the denoised signal based on two wavelet procedures.

 

BACKGROUND

 

This example concerns the analysis of a real-word signal that has been thoroughly analyzed in Misiti,  Misiti, Oppenheim & Poggi (1994).

It is particularly interesting because of the noise introduced whenever a defect is present in the monitoring equipment. The data consist of

measurement of a complex, highly-aggregated plant: the electrical load consumption, sampled minute by minute, over a 3-day period. When

running the script example.m , it loads the original one-dimensional signal, chooses a portion of it, denoises it with two denoising procedures

(recTI with soft thresholding rule and recneighblock)and, finally, plots the denoised estimates.

 

REFERENCES

 

Misiti, M., Misiti, Y., Oppenheim, G. & Poggi, J.-M. (1994). Decomposition en ondelettes et methodes comparatives: etude d'une courbe de charge electrique.  Revue de Statistique Appliquee, 17, 57-77.
